Publications

Voice Cloning and Synthesis Using Deep Learning

International Journal of Innovations in Science & Technology (IJIST)

Published: September 6, 2025

This research explores advanced deep learning architectures for voice cloning and speech synthesis, focusing on enhancing the naturalness, adaptability, and multilingual capabilities of AI-generated speech. It evaluates XTTS_v2, Whisper, and LLaMA 8B models based on transcription accuracy, emotional tone replication, and real-time synthesis, using metrics such as MOS, CER, and SECS. The study proposes a novel pipeline for transforming real-world communication data into expressive, multilingual speech.
